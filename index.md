# The software product builder's oath (alpha)

Software impacts more and more aspects of our existence as a species. As its reach grows, so too do our responsibilities as builders. A builder is anyone who contributes to the existence of the software: a designer, a product owner, a programmer, a quality assurance expert, etc.

The following are not laws, but standards of conduct which define the essentials of honorable behavior for the builder.

I. A builder shall be dedicated to providing competent service with compassion and respect for human dignity.

II. A builder shall deal honestly with users and colleagues, and strive to expose those builders deficient in character or competence, or who engage in fraud or deception.

III. A builder shall respect the law and also recognize a responsibility to seek changes in those requirements which are contrary to the best interests of the user.

IV. Duty to humanity
IV. A builder shall respect the rights of consumers and safeguard their privacy within the constraints of the law.

V. A builder shall continue to study, apply and advance scientific knowledge, make relevant information available to consumers, colleagues, and the public, and use the talents of other builders when indicated.

VI. A builder shall recognize a responsibility to participate in activities contributing to an improved community.

VII. A builder shall refuse to build anything that would be detrimental to the survival of the species.
